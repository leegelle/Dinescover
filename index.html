How it works
Designed to make choosing a restaurant as effortless as tapping a button.
1
Choose your area
Use your current location, or search for a city to plan ahead for travel, date nights, or trips.
2
Tap “Spin”
Dinescover looks up restaurants around that area and randomly suggests one that fits your range.
3
Open, favorite, or share
Open the pick in Apple Maps for directions, add to Favorites, share with friends, or spin again.

Dinescover is designed to feel like a natural extension of iOS — minimal friction, maximum clarity.

Support & FAQ
Have a question or a feature request? We’d love to hear from you.
How do I get support for Dinescover?
You can email us anytime at support@ideacenter.info. We typically respond within 24–48 hours.
Does Dinescover collect personal data?
Dinescover uses your location only to find nearby restaurants. We do not sell your data or track you across other apps. For full details, see our Privacy Policy.
Is Dinescover free?
Yes, Dinescover is free to download and use. Future versions may introduce optional premium features, but the core experience will remain simple and accessible.
