Dinescover – Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Dinescover ("the App") is owned and operated by Ideacenter ("we", "us", "our"). Your privacy is important to us, and this Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data. Dinescover is designed with a privacy-first approach and collects the minimum information needed for the app to function.

1. Information We Collect

Dinescover does not collect, store, or sell any personal information.

A. Location Information (Used Temporarily)

  • Your device’s location is used only while the app is running.
  • We use your location solely to find nearby restaurants via Apple Maps.
  • Your location is never stored, transmitted, or shared.

B. City Search Input

  • When you search for a city, your input is processed locally.
  • No search queries or locations are uploaded or stored on our servers.

C. App Usage Data

We do not collect analytics or usage data. Apple or your device may track anonymized usage statistics according to your system settings.

2. Third-Party Services

Dinescover uses the following Apple frameworks:

  • MapKit – to search and display restaurants and maps.
  • Core Location – to determine your device's approximate location.

These services are governed by Apple’s privacy policies. We do not send any data to third-party servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your location and search inputs are used only to:

  • Find nearby restaurants based on your position or chosen city.
  • Display map previews and distances.

We do not analyze, store, share, or sell any information.

4. Data Storage and Retention

Dinescover does not store personal data on servers. Your History and Favorites are stored locally on your device only and never leave your phone.

5. Children’s Privacy

Dinescover is not directed to children under 13, but it does not collect any personal information regardless of age.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Since we do not collect or store personal data, there is no data to request, modify, or delete.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Updates will be reflected on this page with a revised date.
