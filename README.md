# Dinescover
Dinescover is a native iOS app that makes choosing where to eat simple, fast, and fun. Instead of endlessly scrolling through reviews or maps, Dinescover helps you discover nearby restaurants with a single tap.
With Spin, the app instantly suggests a restaurant near your current location or in any city you choose—perfect whether you’re hungry right now or planning ahead for a trip. Dinescover turns indecision into discovery so you can focus on enjoying the experience. 

✨ Features

🎯 Spin to discover a nearby restaurant (within ~10 miles)
📍 Use your current location or search by city
🗺️ Map preview and distance before you go
❤️ Save favorites to revisit later
🕘 Spin history to review past discoveries
📤 Share picks with friends in just a few taps
📱 Clean, modern design that feels native on iOS

Supported regions currently include North America, Europe, and Australia.
